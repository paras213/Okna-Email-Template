About OKNA

OKNA'S UPVC WINDOWS THAT STANDS WORLD'S TOUGHEST WEATHER


OKNA Designs as an organization holds the ability to contribute to this need by fulfilling an important aspect of this demand by making the best uPVC windows and doors available for this growing ecosystem.

Becoming India’s leading integrated uPVC solutions company, OKNA Designs has a state of art fabrication facility which is spread in an area of around 5500 sq.ft. and is fully equipped with German Technology automatic machines. Having the most comprehensive range and definitive hardware quality, OKNA Designs stands as the fastest growing top quality uPVC windows and doors manufacturer and supplier in India, Specially in Gurgaon and Delhi NCR and is also known to be the largest and the most trusted brand in the uPVC industry.

The product catalogue at OKNA Designs marks the presence of the most prominent technology for thermal (heat), soundproof, and all-weather insulation for doors and windows.
